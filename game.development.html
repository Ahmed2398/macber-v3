Brief

We are transforming concepts into different video games that bring joy and excitement to our users' lives, whether VR or mobile app games.

Beat the parents

A trivia game of 2 players, each player must provide the correct answer before the other to get to the next question. The winner is eligible for a bonus level where they are needed to sing or guess the name of a song, and when finished, a wheel of fortune appears to specify the winner’s prize.

Marathon Pyramid

We created a customer experience mimicking a marathon by executing a giant triad mail for the users to try starting at a low speed. As time goes by, the rate rises and the winner’s name gets displayed on the top 10 list.

Kode Club

We managed to create fun, competitive, and interactive on-ground activations with the help of embedded software and technology sensors simulating football, basketball, cycling, and rugby simulating reality for the users to enjoy announcing the new Kode Club. The rule is to throw the ball as hard as you can and your speed will be calculated and displayed through the screen.

Free Fire

We successfully installed an outstanding digital wheel of fortune for all attendees to enjoy, collect giveaways, and a photo booth where they can take their photos to remember the good times they had.

Juhayna

A virtual game is installed for users to live the experience of a real race in their place with a screen simulating the road and the finish line while the bicycle seats are equipped with speed sensors to allow users to experience the mimic experience of a real race.

Orange

We contributed in the event of announcing the rebranding of Mobinil to Orange by the presence of the memory game, which requires the player to remember the initial furniture locations and recollect their places again to test their memory strengths.

The Groove

We set up a virtual meditating experience created for The Groove Relaxing VR event in Sokhna to transport users to a 3-dimensional relaxation environment where they can meditate, relax and interact with their surroundings.

Surf Simulator:

with the help of Samsung VR gear, the best and easiest tool for human interaction, the attendees of the Mobil 1 event on the North Coast got to test a once-in-a-lifetime experience of riding a mechanical-powered surfboard to showcase how Mobil oil works inside a car engine.

WE Egypt

A virtual journey into embedded reality through a rollercoaster with the help of VR technology transporting the users into one-of-a-kind journeys between majestic mountains with the help of embedded real-time, sensors, and hydraulics.
